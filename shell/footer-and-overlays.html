20 July: force used at Sansad Chalo march

20 July 2026 · Jantar Mantar, Delhi

Police used lathis and tear gas to disperse protesters marching from Jantar Mantar toward Parliament over NEET and CBSE failures. Several protesters and political leaders were detained; official injury and detention totals were not confirmed.
