About / Support

Who maintains this record

LetsFixIndia is maintained by Basith↗ and built to become a sourced public record. It is not a shrine, a slogan, or a rage-feed. Each entry should let you ask three ordinary questions: what happened, who says so, and what is still unresolved.

About me

Independent builder and editor

This is a one-person project with a deliberately public working method: raw notes stay preserved, public entries stay sourced, and corrections are expected. The archive is allowed to be unfinished; it is not allowed to pretend otherwise.

Support

How people can help

Support it with the least glamorous things that make an archive useful: send the missing date, the court order, the official number, or the sentence that is too strong. Translation, source-checking, court-status updates, and careful disagreement all help.
