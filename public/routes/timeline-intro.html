Democracy

A system of government where power is vested in the people, who rule either directly or through freely elected representatives.

In simple words, as Abraham Lincoln said: "Democracy is government of the people, by the people, and for the people."

Pronunciation: dih-MOK-ruh-see (IPA: /dɪˈmɑːkrəsi/) | Etymology: Ancient Greek demokratia (demos "the people" + kratos "power/rule")
